ABOUT US

ReadyUp is a leading innovation and intelligence platform, connecting over 10,000+ tech enthusiasts and entrepreneurs monthly. Founded in 2025, ReadyUp delivers impactful stories, detailed research, and transformative programs for tech leaders.

We've influenced industries with over 2000 articles, 10+ research publications, and dozens of summits hosting top entrepreneurs. Global media such as Wired, BBC, and CNBC follow our insights and reports.

Our ReadyUp Labs team partners with enterprises to craft unique content and reach decision-makers, while our regional initiatives support local tech ecosystems in collaboration with governments.

Through ReadyUp Data, we track thousands of startups and innovations, delivering intelligence that drives better decisions. ReadyUp Plus, our premium membership, empowers thousands with deep insights and special access.
